This file will list all the information that every minigame requires to play and be unique.

*Name: What the minigame will be called. 

*Info: Some information about the game. Example: Spleef is a gametype where you attempt to break the blocks out from underneath players. The last man standing is victorious.

*Base game: What mini game to base general options on. If this is set, the default values will change to the games values. OPTIONS: whatever mini game files are in the folder. example: paintball-minigame.yml

*PvP: whether or not people can hurt each other, info VV
 - enabled: true or false
 - damage modifier: Number to multiply damage by. Will be overridden by VV
 - kit-specific damage modifiers: whether or not kits will have their own damage multipliers. For instance, blaze does *0.8 damage, but sets players on fire. Useful for balancing.
 - health: How much health players should have.
 - kit-specific health: whether or not kits should have different health values.

 - kit-specific invincibility: whether or not kits can toggle invincibility
 - regenerate health: whether or not to regenerate health
 - kit-specific regen: you know the drill.
